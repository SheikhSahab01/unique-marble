Unique Marble

Quality is Our Priority

With over 25 years of experience, we developed a brand name exclusively for export purposes in 2017 under the name of Unique Marble and a sister company, Al Harmain Marble, with the vision of processing and exporting Indian stones at competitive prices while maintaining quality standards.

As we keep our promise to our customers that "Quality is our Priority" we have gained huge success and received plenty of positive feedback from happy customers in more than 20 countries around the world who are importing various granite, marble, sandstone, and landscaping stones from us.

WHAT WE DO & ARE CAPABLE OF!

Quality is Our Priority

Today, we are exporting more than 100 types of Indian granites and marbles in different finishes, sizes, and thicknesses.
We also offer landscaping stones in the form of granite and limestone for steps, coping, pavers, pier caps, kerbs, veneers, and corners.
We also offer dual-sided polished granites in thicknesses ranging from 40 mm up to 200 mm in all the granites that we export.
We also offer handicrafts made from marble, granite, and sandstone, like bathtubs, pedestal basins, countertop basins, flower pots, fountains, and our hot-selling granite benches.
